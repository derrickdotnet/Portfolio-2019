MyBeesbox Online Gifting Service


MyBeesbox.com is an E-Commerce website that allows users to choose or create a customized gift box for any occasion.

My duties were to create a prototype website in WordPress using WooCommerce, with no content from our client; only an idea of what the site would be.

WordPress Mockups



I felt and understood the vibe of our client and used that to build the initial site. She was bubbly, kind, and wore vibrant colors each time we talked in person. I used stock images from Adobe Stock and WooCommerce to help paint what the site will look like. Anthropologie.com and LushUSA.com both had user experiences, aesthetics, and products are sources of inspiration she drew from.

Designing the site with not a much to start with and while learning WordPress was a challenge I was excited to face. I spent a lot of time teaching myself the programs and techniques to make better designs and be more efficient.

Before developing the site, I made sketches and wireframes to help hone in what the final prototype will have, look, and feel.
